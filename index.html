Why Valtrix

One partner. Entire region.

Entering CEE is complex. Fragmented retail, local regulations, language barriers, and different consumer behaviors in every market. Most brands either go it alone — slowly and expensively — or partner with local distributors who lack the full picture.

Valtrix is different. We're a platform, not just a distributor. We combine B2B distribution with D2C infrastructure, local marketing expertise, and on-the-ground event presence. One relationship gives you access to everything you need to succeed in the region.

We think like brand operators, not middlemen. Your success is our growth.
